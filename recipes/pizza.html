Pizza

Description

Pizza is a savory dish of Italian origin consisting of a usually round, flattened base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients which is then baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil, plus more for bowl
  • 4 cups bread flour
  • 2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • Vegetable oil, for pan
  • All-purpose flour, for dusting
  • Pizza toppings

Directions

  1. Combine the water, sugar, and yeast in the bowl of a stand mixer and mix with the dough hook until combined. Let sit for 10 minutes.
  2. Add the flour, salt, and olive oil, and mix on low speed until combined. Knead on low speed until the dough is smooth and elastic, about 10 minutes.
  3. Transfer to a well-oiled bowl, cover with plastic, and let rise until doubled in size, about 1 1/2 hours. Press it with your finger to see if it's done; an indent should remain.
  4. Turn the dough out onto a well-floured surface. Divide the dough into 2 equal pieces. Cover loosely and let rest for 10 minutes.
  5. Use immediately or refrigerate for up to 24 hours.
